Curriculum

Mentee/Mentor Pairing

Rising sophomores and juniors will be able to apply for a major they would like to study/learn about. They will then apply and be paired up with an undergraduate student who is currently majoring in that major.

How ASH works

When a high school student and a college student both input a common major, our algorithm will pair them up together. After these two people are paired, they will meet once a week

What happens when you get in

When you get into our program, you will be able to learn any major you want by the time college comes around. You're allowed to apply as many times as you want for different majors. After this program, you will understand much more on whether you'd like to pursue a certain major or not. For the mentor, you will be able to gain college credit for helping out a mentee.
